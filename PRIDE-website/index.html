Mission Statement:

For the school year 2023-2024, our focus for Pride at Parker is to create a larger space for allies to band together and support the LGBTQ+ community as a whole. We recognize the importance of allyship and want to ensure that everyone feels included and supported in our efforts. The four of us, Amy, Gabby, Elise, and Anna, aim to create a safe and welcoming environment for all students, regardless of their sexual orientation or gender identity. Our goal is to increase the variety of events and create more opportunities for education and awareness about LGBTQ+ issues. We will also continue our tradition of Pride Week, which includes designated days for different members of the LGBTQ+ community and fun colors to represent the Pride flag. We will host focused meetings each day to educate students about gay and queer history and the experiences of members of the community. In addition to supporting the LGBTQ+ community, we also want to ensure that the Parker community is involved in our efforts. We will send out regular updates and announcements about our activities and provide feedback forms to solicit input from our peers. We are committed to taking everyone's suggestions into consideration and making sure that everyone feels heard and included. As a team, we are dedicated to creating a safe and supportive space for all members of the Parker community. We bring diverse experiences and perspectives to the table, and we are excited to work together to make this year's Pride at Parker a success.
